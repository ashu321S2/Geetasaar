GeetaSaar:

Welcome to our website

The project "GeetaSaar" integrates the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita into modern life. By translating these ancient teachings of Bhagavad Gita into practical solutions, it offers a holistic framework for personal growth, ethical decision-making, and spiritual evolution. This seamless blend of tradition and relevance helps individuals navigate life with clarity, purpose, and resilience.




“In the timeless words of the Gita, discover strength in truth, peace in duty, and divinity in the self — this is not just scripture, it is a guide to life.”


Whatever has happened was for the best. Whatever is happening is for the best. Whatever is yet to happen will also be for the best. Why do you mourn what is lost? What did you bring into this world that you believe is now gone? What have you created that you fear is destroyed? You arrived empty-handed; all that you possess was received here. Whatever you have taken, you have taken from the Divine. Whatever you have given, you have offered to the Divine. Change is the eternal law of the universe. What you perceive as death is merely another form of life. One moment you may gain riches, the next you may lose them. Let go of distinctions like mine and yours, big and small. Free yourself from these illusions, and you will realize that everything belongs to you, and you belong to all. Elevate yourself through your own efforts; do not degrade your being. The self is both the greatest friend and the greatest enemy of oneself. Fulfill your duties sincerely, for action is superior to inaction. Without action, even the body cannot be sustained. Whenever virtue declines and vice prevails, I manifest on Earth. To protect the righteous, destroy evil, and restore the balance of dharma, I descend age after age.
